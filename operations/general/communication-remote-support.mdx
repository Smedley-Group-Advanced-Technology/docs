## Overview

This SOP provides clarity on what information is needed to get assistance from remote support, the most appropriate methods to communicate, and what outcomes should be expected.

**Who:** Anyone on-event, Remote Support

**When:** Any time on-event

---

## Prerequisites

- Kart Issue Recording
- Operational Issue Recording

---

## Procedure

<Steps>
  <Step title="Confirm remote support contact">
    The person, contact details, and availability of remote support will be communicated to each hub pre-event.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the appropriate communication channel">
    Use the channel that matches the priority of your issue:

    | Channel | Response Time | Priority |
    |--|--|--|
    | Teams channel messaging | 10 minutes | Medium |
    | Individual Teams messaging | 10 minutes | Medium |
    | Teams call | Immediate | High |
    | WhatsApp messaging | 10 minutes | Medium |
    | WhatsApp calling | Immediate | High |
    | Email | 30 minutes | Low |
    | Discord | 10 minutes | Medium |
    | Kart Issue Tracker | 10 minutes | Medium |

    <Note>
      The **Kart Issue Tracker** should be the primary route of communication so that issues can be clearly tracked and recorded.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sending a data file to remote support">
    When sending a data file, the following information must be attached:

    - **Kart number**
    - **Battery nodes and order** — from right-hand side: top left, bottom left
    - **Data type:**
      - `Run` — a standard on-track session
      - `Charge` — a standard pit lane charge
      - `Test` — a specific on-track or charge session with the intention of fault diagnosis
      - `Log` — a short file to capture a snapshot of data
    - **Number**

    <Note>
      Example format: `"Kart EK311, nodes 012, charge 7"`
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Raising a non-kart issue">
    If the issue is not kart-related, use the **Teams channel** and create a new conversation for each issue:

    - Use an appropriate heading that describes the issue
    - Tag whoever is providing remote support to ensure they are notified
    - When the issue is resolved, add `[Resolved]` to the conversation heading
  </Step>
  <Step title="Urgent issues">
    If the issue is urgent, a **Teams call** is the recommended method.
  </Step>
</Steps>
