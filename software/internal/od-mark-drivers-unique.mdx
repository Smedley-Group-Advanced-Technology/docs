## Overview

As the FKL championships grow, it is increasingly likely that multiple drivers will share the same name. Where the system flags two drivers as duplicates but they are actually separate, unique customers, their profiles must be marked as distinct to prevent them from being incorrectly flagged in future.

---

## Prerequisites

- OD access

---

## Procedure

<Warning>
  This process is not reversible so ensure you are making the correct steps before confirming.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the Drivers tab">
    Go to the Operator Dashboard and navigate to the **Drivers** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the duplicate records">
    Click **Duplicates** to filter the driver list and locate the driver cards flagged as duplicates.

    ![Duplicates view on the Operator Dashboard](/images/software/internal/od-duplicates.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the snowflake icon">
    Click the snowflake icon labelled **Mark driver as distinct** on the first driver. This will show a red cancel on the original driver and a green confirm button on all other drivers in the duplicate group.

    ![Mark driver as distinct button](/images/software/internal/od-mark-as-unique.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the second unique driver">
    Click the green confirm button on the second driver to indicate they are a distinct, unique customer from the first.

    ![Mark both drivers as distinct](/images/software/internal/od-mark-both-as-distinct.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the action">
    Confirm the action on the pop-up window to complete the process.

    ![Confirmation pop-up](/images/software/internal/od-confirm-unique.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
