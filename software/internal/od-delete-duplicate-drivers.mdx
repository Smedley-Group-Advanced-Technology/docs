## Overview

Duplicate driver records must be resolved to ensure data integrity across the platform. This SOP covers how to identify duplicates, select the correct record to delete, and merge any associated tickets into the surviving profile.

---

## Prerequisites

- OD access

---

## Procedure

<Warning>
  This process is not reversible so ensure you are making the correct steps before confirming.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the Drivers tab">
    Go to the Operator Dashboard and navigate to the **Drivers** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the duplicate records">
    Click **Duplicates** to filter the driver list and locate the duplicate driver cards.

    ![Duplicates view on the Operator Dashboard](/images/software/internal/od-duplicates.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the driver to delete">
    Identify which of the two duplicate records should be deleted.

    <Note>
      Keep the record that has the correct associated user, parent, tickets, and history. Delete the other one.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Delete Driver">
    On the duplicate record you wish to remove, click the red **Delete Driver** button.
	
	![Delete driver button](/images/software/internal/od-delete-driver.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check for associated tickets">
    Two outcomes are possible:

    **No tickets** — the driver can be deleted immediately with no further action required.
	
	![No tickets found for selected driver](/images/software/internal/od-no-tickets.png)

    **Tickets present** — the tickets must be moved to another driver profile before the deletion can be completed. Continue with the steps below.
	
	![Tickets present for selected driver](/images/software/internal/od-tickets.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search for the driver to merge into">
    Search for the name of the driver you wish to merge the tickets into. This should be the other duplicate — the one you are keeping.
	
	![Move tickets confirmation window](/images/software/internal/od-move-tickets.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the driver">
    Double-click on the driver profile to merge the tickets into.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the merge">
    The red **Confirm** button will now become active. Click it to complete the merge and delete the duplicate.
	
	![Move tickets confirmation button active](/images/software/internal/od-move-tickets-confirm.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
